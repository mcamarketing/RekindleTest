## üìù **COPYWRITING PRINCIPLES FOR EACH SECTION**

### **1. HERO (Awareness Stage)**
**Goal:** Grab attention + establish credibility  
**Copy Focus:**
- Headline: Clear outcome (Revive Cold Leads ‚Üí Booked Meetings)
- Subheadline: Mechanism teaser (AI + Performance Pricing)
- Trust: SOC 2, GDPR, Pilot badges
- CTA: Low commitment ("See How" not "Start Trial")

### **2. PROBLEM (Agitation Stage)**
**Goal:** Make them feel the pain they're currently ignoring  
**Copy Focus:**
- Quantify the hidden cost (¬£100K+ rotting)
- "You're not alone" - industry-wide problem
- Emotional trigger: wasted investment, missed opportunities
- Transition: "But it doesn't have to be this way..."

### **3. SOLUTION (Hope Stage)**
**Goal:** Position the mechanism as inevitable/obvious  
**Copy Focus:**
- "The only way to revive cold leads at scale"
- AI + Multi-Channel positioning
- Frame as evolution, not revolution
- Transition: "Here's exactly how it works..."

### **4. HOW IT WORKS (Understanding Stage)**
**Goal:** Remove confusion, make it feel easy  
**Copy Focus:**
- 3 simple steps (visual timeline)
- Emphasize speed: "2 minutes to upload"
- Show they stay in control
- Transition: "But what makes this actually convert?"

### **5. PROOF (Belief Stage)**
**Goal:** Prove this actually works (features as proof)  
**Copy Focus:**
- Multi-Channel Strategy (not just email)
- AI Timing Engine (proof of sophistication)
- You control everything (removes fear)
- Transition: "Don't just take our word for it..."

### **6. SOCIAL PROOF (Validation Stage)**
**Goal:** Show others are winning with this  
**Copy Focus:**
- Specific metrics from pilot customers
- Industry names (if allowed, or "FTSE 100 SaaS company")
- Result-focused (meetings booked, pipeline ¬£¬£¬£)
- Transition: "Here's what this costs you..."

### **7. PRICING (Evaluation Stage)**
**Goal:** Make price feel like a steal vs. value  
**Copy Focus:**
- Two-part pricing (transparent breakdown)
- ROI calculator (make it tangible)
- Compare to alternatives (SDR salary = ¬£50K/year)
- "80% of spend is performance-based"
- Transition: "Still on the fence? Here's our guarantee..."

### **8. RISK REVERSAL (Objection Stage)**
**Goal:** Remove every last objection  
**Copy Focus:**
- 100% Money-Back Guarantee (performance fee)
- No-Show Refund Policy
- Cancel Anytime
- "You only pay when we deliver"
- Transition: "But you need to act now..."

### **9. URGENCY (FOMO Stage)**
**Goal:** Create scarcity, make them act NOW  
**Copy Focus:**
- Pilot Program (limited spots)
- Pricing locked for 6 months (grandfathered)
- Application required (exclusivity)
- "Spots filling fast"
- Transition: "Ready to stop wasting ¬£100K+?"

### **10. FINAL CTA (Close Stage)**
**Goal:** Make the ask crystal clear  
**Copy Focus:**
- Outcome-focused headline ("Stop Letting ¬£100K+ Die")
- Primary CTA: "Apply for Pilot Access"
- Secondary CTA: "Calculate Your ROI" (low commitment)
- Trust reminders (SOC 2, GDPR)
