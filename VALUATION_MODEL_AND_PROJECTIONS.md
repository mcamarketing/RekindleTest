**Key Success Factors:**
1. **Product velocity:** Ship fast, iterate faster
2. **Viral PLG:** 2.3M users → 180K paying (7.8% conversion)
3. **AI native:** Built for AI-first workflows
4. **Developer focus:** Clear ICP (developers building apps)
5. **Usage-based revenue:** High ARPU from power users

## 1.4 Unit Economics Summary

**Formula:** LTV = (ARPU × Gross Margin) / Monthly Churn Rate

**Year 1:**
- ARPU: $850
- Gross Margin: 82%
- Churn: 3%
- **LTV = ($850 × 0.82) / 0.03 = $23,233**

**Year 2:**
- ARPU: $1,050
- Gross Margin: 85%
- Churn: 2%
- **LTV = ($1,050 × 0.85) / 0.02 = $44,625**

**Year 3:**
- ARPU: $1,250
- Gross Margin: 87%
- Churn: 2%
- **LTV = ($1,250 × 0.87) / 0.02 = $54,375**

# SECTION 2: STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

## 2.1 Critical Path to Beating Lovable's Trajectory

### What Lovable Did Right (Lessons to Apply)

1. **Product Velocity:** Shipped MVP → $100M ARR in 8 months
   - **Rekindle Action:** Ship production-ready app in 2 weeks (not months)
   - **Metric:** Week 1-2 = environment + infrastructure + deliverability
   - **Goal:** First paying customer by Week 4

2. **Viral PLG:** 2.3M users → 180K paying (7.8% conversion)
   - **Rekindle Action:** Launch freemium tier by Month 3
   - **Metric:** 100 leads free, 1 campaign/month, basic AI
   - **Goal:** 10% free-to-paid conversion (beat Lovable's 7.8%)

3. **Usage-Based Pricing:** $1-$50 per AI task = high ARPU from power users
   - **Rekindle Action:** Performance fees (2.5% ACV) already implements this
   - **Metric:** Track meetings booked → revenue attributed
   - **Goal:** $500+ avg performance fee per customer

4. **Developer Focus:** Clear ICP (developers) = efficient GTM
   - **Rekindle Action:** Focus on 1-2 verticals initially (SaaS, Real Estate)
   - **Metric:** Win rate > 30% in target vertical
   - **Goal:** Dominate niche before expanding

5. **Enterprise Customers:** Klarna, HubSpot, Photoroom = credibility + high ACV
   - **Rekindle Action:** Land 1-2 logo customers by Month 6
   - **Metric:** Enterprise deal size > $3K/month
   - **Goal:** 3-5 case studies with Fortune 500 logos

---

### What Rekindle Must Do Differently

1. **Faster Sales Cycle:** B2B enterprise has 30-90 day sales cycle (vs Lovable's self-serve)
   - **Solution:** Offer free pilot (3 months) → convert to performance-based pricing
   - **Metric:** Pilot-to-paid conversion > 70%
   - **Impact:** Reduce risk for prospects, prove ROI before payment
